1.

At Q42, technology is our lens for creativity. So our logo itself is also technology: a code logo.

2.

Logos have always changed form with the times. From lead type to black-and-white xerox-friendly to full-color gradients. But logos also have been static. The Q42 logo is interactive, because our medium is interactive.

3.

Q42 doesn't have style guides: we have no need to constrain creativity in our company. But we do like to make things easy. On this page, we've put all the stuff you need to use our logo.

Nuts & bolts

The Q42 logo is all bezier curves. The control points and coordinates are in the blueprint below. The Q-Green is #84bc2d.
